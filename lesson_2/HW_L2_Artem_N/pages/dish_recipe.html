Olivier salad

Recipe:

Components:

  1. Boiled potatoes - 400 g
  2. Boiled carrots - 150 g
  3. Boiled eggs - 6 pcs
  4. Pickled cucumbers - 300 g
  5. Doctor's sausage - 300 g
  6. Mayonnaise - 300 ml
  7. Canned green peas - 1 jar
  8. Green onion - 1 bunch (36 g)
  9. Salt (optional) - to taste
  10. Ground pepper (optional) - to taste

Cooking:

  • Boil the potatoes and carrots in their skins (20-25 minutes; check the readiness of the vegetables with a knife or fork), cool. Hard boil the eggs (8-9 minutes after boiling), cool in cold water.
  • Peel the potatoes and carrots.
  • Slice the pickles. Place them on the bottom of a deep bowl.
  • Place canned peas on top of the cucumbers.
  • Cut the boiled sausage into small cubes and add to a bowl with cucumbers and peas.
  • Cut the carrots into small cubes and add to the bowl.
  • Cut the potatoes into small cubes and add to the rest of the Olivier salad ingredients.
  • Chop the eggs into small cubes and add to the salad with sausage, peas and vegetables.
  • Season Olivier with mayonnaise mixed with sour cream. Salt and pepper.
  • Stir the salad and let it sit for a few minutes.
